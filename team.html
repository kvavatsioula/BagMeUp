BagMeUp's backstory


The idea of BagMeUp emerged for the objectives of the course "Electronic Commerce and Internet Applications" of the Dept. of Management Science and Technology, Athens University of Economics and Business in March, 2019. The team was formed and we started working on that project. From then on, BagMeUp has been supported by the Athens Center of Enterpreneurship and Innovation (ACEin), an incubation center based in Athens, Greece that offers support to potential young entrepreneurs in order to develop innovative business ideas and bring them to the market. Now, BagMeup operates in pilot version and offers luggage storage services free of charge, partnering up with hosting points all over in Athens.
